## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I register myself?
**A:** No. Only administrators can register new users. Ask your admin.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md](./REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md) ‚Üí "Security First"

---

### Q: What's a strong password?
**A:** Mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, special characters. Minimum 6 characters.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md](./REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md) ‚Üí "Password Tips"

---

### Q: What's the difference between User and Manager roles?
**A:** Managers can access advanced reports and approvals. Users can only create loans.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md](./REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md) ‚Üí "User Roles Explained"

---

### Q: Error: "Passwords do not match"
**A:** Your password fields are different. Re-enter both passwords carefully.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md](./REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md) ‚Üí "Error Reference Table"

---

### Q: How do I reset a user's password?
**A:** That's not done in registration. Contact your IT administrator.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md](./REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md) ‚Üí "Password Reset for Registered Users"

---

### Q: Can I register multiple users at once?
**A:** No, register one at a time. The form resets after each registration.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md](./REGISTRATION_QUICK_REFERENCE.md) ‚Üí "Multiple User Registration"

---

### Q: What happens to passwords after registration?
**A:** They're hashed (encrypted) and cannot be reversed. For security.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_TECHNICAL_GUIDE.md](./REGISTRATION_TECHNICAL_GUIDE.md) ‚Üí "Password Security"

---

### Q: How do I check if a user was registered?
**A:** Try logging in with their credentials. If it works, they're registered.

**More info:** [REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md](./REGISTRATION_GUIDE.md) ‚Üí "After Registration"

---
